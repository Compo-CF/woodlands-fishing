Permits & Rules

Texas Freshwater License

Required for anyone 17 or older fishing in public Texas waters. Annual freshwater package: $30 resident · $58 non-resident · $12 senior. No license required in a Texas State Park (bank/pier).

Buy a license at TPWD ↗

The Woodlands Township Ponds

The Township does NOT issue its own fishing permit — a state license is enough. Most Township ponds are catch-and-release only. The exceptions where you can keep fish are Lake Woodlands, Lake Paloma, and Bear Branch Reservoir.

Township Parks & Rec ↗

Lake Conroe (SJRA)

Standard TPWD license — no extra SJRA permit. Largemouth bass 16″ min (vs. 14″ statewide). 5-fish daily bag combined.
