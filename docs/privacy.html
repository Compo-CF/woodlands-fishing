Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 26, 2026

Woodlands Fishing ("the App") is a small, independent iOS application that helps users find fishing spots in and around The Woodlands, Texas. This policy explains exactly what information the App accesses and, more importantly, what it does not do with it.

Information the App accesses

The App requests access to your device's location, and only while the App is open. Your location is used for two purposes:

  • To display your position on the map alongside nearby fishing spots
  • To sort the list of spots by distance from where you are

What the App does not do

  • The App does not transmit your location to any server
  • The App does not store your location data anywhere — not on the device, not in the cloud
  • The App does not share your location with any third party
  • The App does not contain analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
  • The App does not collect personal information such as your name, email, or device identifiers
  • The App does not require account creation or login

Apple's MapKit

The App uses Apple's MapKit framework to display maps and to hand off directions to Apple Maps. Map tile rendering and directions are handled by Apple. See Apple's privacy policy for details on how Apple handles map-related data.

Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
